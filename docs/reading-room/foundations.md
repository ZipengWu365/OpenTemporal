---
title: Foundational Literature
description: Classic references that shaped time-series analysis.
---

- Box, G.E.P., Jenkins, G.M., and Reinsel, G.C. *Time Series Analysis: Forecasting and Control* (Wiley). Standard reference for ARIMA and state-space thinking.
- Chatfield, C. *The Analysis of Time Series: An Introduction* (CRC Press). Accessible introduction with emphasis on diagnostics.
- Brockwell, P.J. and Davis, R.A. *Introduction to Time Series and Forecasting* (Springer). Rigorous treatment of stochastic processes and inference.
- Hamilton, J.D. *Time Series Analysis* (Princeton University Press). Macroeconomic perspective with structural models.
- Shumway, R.H. and Stoffer, D.S. *Time Series Analysis and Its Applications* (Springer). Blends time and frequency domain views with R examples.

All of these texts are widely cited and available through academic libraries or official publishers.
